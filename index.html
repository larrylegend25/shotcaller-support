ShotCaller Support

Help for ShotCaller subscriptions, Apple Watch shot logging, and troubleshooting.

Restore Purchases

  1. Open ShotCaller.
  2. On the ShotCaller Pro screen or in Settings, tap Restore Purchases.
  3. Make sure you are signed in with the Apple ID used for the original subscription.

If restore does not unlock ShotCaller Pro, confirm the subscription is active in your Apple ID subscriptions and try again.

Manage or Cancel Subscription

  1. Open the iOS Settings app.
  2. Tap your name at the top.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Select ShotCaller.
  5. Choose the option to manage or cancel your subscription.

Subscription billing and cancellation are handled through Apple.

Apple Watch Troubleshooting

  • Keep the iPhone app open during a tracking session for the most reliable Watch syncing.
  • Confirm Bluetooth is enabled and the Watch is connected to your iPhone.
  • Restart both the iPhone app and the Watch app if shot actions stop appearing.
  • Make sure ShotCaller is installed on both iPhone and Apple Watch.
  • If Pocket Lock is enabled, use the Watch controls for shot logging while phone controls are locked.

General Troubleshooting

  • Update ShotCaller to the latest version available from the App Store or TestFlight.
  • Force quit and reopen the app if the interface becomes unresponsive.
  • Check your internet connection before starting a subscription purchase or restore.
  • Restart your iPhone if purchase or Watch connection issues continue.
